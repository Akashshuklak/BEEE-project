BASIC ELCTRICAL ENGINEERING

What is EE ?

Electrical engineering is a discipline of engineering that deals with the study, design, development, and application of electrical systems, devices, and technologies. It encompasses a wide range of topics related to electricity, electromagnetism, and electronics. Electrical engineers work on the generation, transmission, distribution, and utilization of electrical power, as well as the design and development of electrical and electronic devices.

Applications

Electrical engineering has a significant impact on our day-to-day lives. Here are some examples of how electrical engineering is involved in various aspects of our daily lives:

Power Supply:
Electrical engineers are responsible for designing, operating, and maintaining the power generation, transmission, and distribution systems that provide electricity to our homes and offices.

Lightning and ilumination:
Electrical engineers contribute to the design and implementation of lighting systems in homes, workplaces, and public spaces.

Home appliances:
Electrical engineers play a crucial role in designing and developing home appliances like refrigerators, washing machines, air conditioners, televisions, and kitchen appliances

Communication and networking:
Electrical engineers contribute to the design and development of telecommunications systems, including mobile networks, internet infrastructure, and data communication technologies.

Information Technology:
Electrical engineers are involved in the design and development of computer hardware, including processors, memory systems, and networking devices.
